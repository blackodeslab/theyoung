Summary

CothuPb is a mobile application developed for Android and IOS. I'm using the power of the Ionic, Angular, Capacitor and Cordova frameworks to build a complete platform that allows users, particularly those based in Africa, to organise contributions for all kinds of activities within their respective groups.

The application exploits the advantages of mobile money to facilitate transactions and automatic reporting. It will be operational in Benin for MTN MoMo and Moov Money. It will also be available in the Democratic Republic of Congo for Vodacom M-Pesa, Airtel Money and Orange Money. But don't worry, it will eventually be available in all African countries where mobile money is the main means of payment.
